ORBIIT Card Design Options

Minimal & Clean Styles

Minimal Outlined

Clean, sharp edges with offset shadow on hover. Perfect for a professional, modern look that matches your site's minimal aesthetic.

OUTLINED

Matches your logo style with outlined text and fill-on-hover effect. Creates visual consistency across your brand elements.

OUTLINED BLUR

Same outlined style but with subtle background blur effect. Creates depth while maintaining the clean outlined aesthetic.

Grid Pattern

Incorporates your site's grid background pattern subtly on hover. Creates a cohesive design language throughout your site.

Animated & Interactive Styles

Orbital Grid

Features a rotating grid pattern background that suggests orbital motion, perfectly complementing your 3D animation theme.

Floating Sphere

Includes a floating circle element that echoes the spheres in your animation. Subtle movement creates visual interest.

3D Perspective

Uses CSS 3D transforms to create depth on hover, maintaining the dimensional theme of your orbital animation.

Unique & Creative Styles

Neon Outline

Glowing border effect that activates on hover, creating a futuristic tech aesthetic perfect for innovative projects.

Orbit Path

Features a subtle orbital ring that rotates around the card, directly inspired by your animation's orbit mechanics.

Typewriter

Text appears with a typewriter effect on hover, adding dynamic storytelling to your content presentation.

Bold & Modern Styles

Glassmorphism

Modern glass effect with blur and transparency, creating depth while maintaining your clean aesthetic.

Split Reveal

Card splits and reveals content with a sliding animation, perfect for showcasing before/after or dual concepts.

Magnetic Hover

Elements within the card react to mouse position, creating an interactive magnetic field effect.
